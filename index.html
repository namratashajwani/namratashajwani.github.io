About
A designer who thinks in visuals, stories, and space.
Hey — I'm Namrata, a multidisciplinary designer based in Boston, MA. I work across XR & immersive experiences, spatial environments, brand identity, and social content.

For me, design is never just visual — it's how ideas find their form. I'm drawn to work that sits at the intersection of storytelling and structure — where a well-considered layout or an immersive environment can genuinely shift how someone feels.
