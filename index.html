About
A designer who thinks in visuals, stories, and space.
Design, to me, feels a bit like a game of Tetris — where ideas, intuition, and structure find their place through thoughtful choices in typography, layout, color, and form.

I'm a multidisciplinary designer, working across XR & immersive experiences, spatial environments, brand identity, and social content creation and design. I'm drawn to work that sits at the intersection of storytelling and structure.

Outside of design, I enjoy spending time outdoors hiking and running, and meeting new people. I stay motivated and inspired by attending industry conferences, participating in hackathons, and constantly exploring new tools, technologies, and creative practices.
