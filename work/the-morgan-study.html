The Project
Visual study and documentation.

This project is a printed editorial booklet documenting three objects from The Morgan Library & Museum in Manhattan. Each object was photographed on-site, researched, and written about through a personal lens of observation and analysis.

The process involved photographing artifacts, maintaining notes in the tour, editing and color-correcting images, and designing the full layout with considered typography, grid structure, and print-ready output. The final deliverable was a physical printed booklet.
